## 🎯 Business Problem

AtliQ Media is producing a one-hour election special on the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election.

Unlike traditional election coverage focused on debates and opinions, the objective was to build a completely data-driven story using only Election Commission of India (ECI) data.

The challenge was not analysis alone.

The challenge was identifying the most important stories hidden within 234 constituencies and presenting them in a way that a general audience could understand instantly.

---

## 📊 Executive Summary

| Metric                        | Value       |
| ----------------------------- | ----------- |
| Constituencies Analysed       | 234         |
| Seats Changed Hands           | 163         |
| Flip Rate                     | 69.7%       |
| Average Winning Margin (2026) | 16.8K Votes |

### Key Findings

* 163 constituencies changed hands between 2021 and 2026.
* Nearly 7 out of every 10 seats elected a different party.
* Average winning margins declined significantly compared to 2021.
* Electoral shifts were visible across all six regions of Tamil Nadu.
* Reserved constituencies largely followed statewide voting patterns.

# 📖 The Three Stories

## 1️⃣ The Geographic Story

How did political control shift across Tamil Nadu's six regions?

### Key Insight

Every region experienced measurable change, but the scale of change varied significantly across the state.

---

## 2️⃣ The Flip Story

Which constituencies changed hands?

Who gained?

Who lost?

### Key Insight

163 seats changed hands, creating one of the largest constituency-level shifts in recent Tamil Nadu elections.

---

## 3️⃣ The Margin Story

Did voters deliver decisive victories or closely contested races?

### Key Insight

Average winning margins declined substantially, indicating a more competitive election environment.
